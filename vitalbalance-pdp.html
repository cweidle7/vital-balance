NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme central to mitochondrial energy metabolism and DNA biology. Levels decline with age. This formula pairs nicotinamide riboside (NR) with a synergistic methylation matrix, providing each cell with the precursors required for endogenous NAD+ biosynthesis.

Formulated alongside practicing clinicians, manufactured to pharmaceutical-grade standards, and bottled in glass for substrate integrity.

  • Nicotinamide Riboside (NR) — 300 mg · the most studied NAD+ precursor.
  • Trimethylglycine (TMG) — 250 mg · supports methylation balance.
  • Pterostilbene — 50 mg · activates SIRT1 longevity pathway.
  • Magnesium Glycinate — 75 mg · ATP cofactor.

Vegetable cellulose capsule. No fillers, dyes, magnesium stearate, or synthetic flow agents.
