NAD+ is the molecular currency of cellular repair. As we age, levels decline — and with them, the body's capacity to fuel mitochondria, repair DNA, and clear cellular debris. This formula pairs nicotinamide riboside (NR) with a synergistic methylation matrix, giving every cell the precursors it needs to make NAD+ on its own.

Built alongside the practitioners who prescribe it, refined through six rounds of clinical testing, and bottled in glass — because anything we ask the body to absorb deserves a vessel that respects it.

  • Nicotinamide Riboside (NR) — 300 mg · the most studied NAD+ precursor.
  • Trimethylglycine (TMG) — 250 mg · supports methylation balance.
  • Pterostilbene — 50 mg · activates SIRT1 longevity pathway.
  • Magnesium Glycinate — 75 mg · ATP cofactor.

Vegetable cellulose capsule. No fillers, dyes, magnesium stearate, or synthetic flow agents.

Two capsules with breakfast or your first meal of the day. Best taken with healthy fats for optimal absorption.

For practitioner-supervised protocols, dosing may scale to 4–6 capsules daily, divided across meals.

NAD+ depletion is one of the twelve recognized hallmarks of aging. Restoring it via dietary precursors has been shown in multiple human trials to improve mitochondrial function, sleep quality, and metabolic markers — without the bioavailability and methylation drain of older NMN-only formulas.
