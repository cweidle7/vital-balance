Regulatory Compliance

Vital Balance strictly adheres to all applicable federal and state regulations governing the sale and distribution of HCT/P allografts and research peptides. All products are sold exclusively to licensed healthcare professionals and qualified researchers.

Required Disclaimers

The following mandatory disclaimers appear on all Vital Balance product pages, order forms, and purchase confirmations. By placing an order, customers acknowledge and agree to these terms.

  • For Licensed Healthcare Professionals Only — not for consumer sale or self-administration
  • Research Peptides: For Research Purposes Only — not for human consumption outside a licensed, supervised protocol
  • HCT/P Allografts: For use by licensed healthcare professionals in a clinical setting, in compliance with 21 CFR Part 1271
  • Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease
  • Individual results may vary; no outcomes are guaranteed

FDA-registered HCT/P establishment under 21 CFR Part 1271 — establishment registration is not product approval.

Intended Use Guidelines

HCT/P Allografts

Vital Balance HCT/P products are human tissue and cellular products distributed in compliance with 21 CFR Part 1271. They are intended exclusively for licensed healthcare professionals in clinical settings, as part of established patient treatment protocols.

Proper handling, storage, and administration must comply with all applicable federal and state regulations governing human tissue products.

Research Peptides

All peptide products are intended for laboratory and clinical research by qualified researchers or licensed healthcare providers. These products are not FDA-approved for therapeutic use and are not intended for human consumption outside a licensed, supervised research protocol.

Purchasers must have appropriate training in research protocols, safety procedures, and applicable regulations.

Prohibited Uses

The following uses are strictly prohibited:

  • Human self-administration or unsupervised consumption
  • Veterinary applications or animal treatment
  • Sale or redistribution to consumers or unlicensed parties
  • Dietary supplement or wellness product formulation
  • Clinical trials or medical procedures outside a licensed setting
  • Any non-research commercial application

Regulatory Standards

FDA / 21 CFR Part 1271

Vital Balance HCT/P allograft products are sourced from and distributed through an FDA-registered establishment under 21 CFR Part 1271. Establishment registration is not product approval. All products comply with applicable donor eligibility, processing, storage, and labeling requirements.

FTC Marketing Guidelines

All product descriptions and marketing materials use scientifically accurate language appropriate for research and clinical audiences. Vital Balance does not make unsupported therapeutic or wellness claims, in compliance with FTC guidelines applicable to research chemical suppliers.

Provider Verification

All Vital Balance accounts are verified as licensed healthcare professionals or qualified researchers before product access is granted. Applications require professional license credentials, which are reviewed and retained on file in accordance with record-keeping requirements.

Purchase Acknowledgment & Record Keeping

At the time of each order, buyers confirm: "I am purchasing these products solely for legitimate professional or research use by a licensed practitioner." All order records, provider credentials, and purchase confirmations are maintained to support regulatory audit requirements.

Quality Assurance

All Vital Balance products meet specified purity standards (≥98% or ≥99%) and ship with a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from an accredited third-party laboratory. Every CoA includes:

  • Chemical name and molecular structure
  • Molecular weight and amino acid sequence (where applicable)
  • Purity percentage — HPLC and MS verified
  • Storage conditions and handling requirements
  • Research applications and intended use
  • Lot number and expiration date
